## 🎯 **Trading Strategies**

### **1. Momentum Scalping**
- **Entry**: Price acceleration > 0.1% with volume confirmation
- **Exit**: Trailing stop at 50% of initial risk
- **Hold Time**: 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Win Rate**: 65-75%

### **2. Mean Reversion Scalping**
- **Entry**: RSI < 25 or > 75 with order flow confirmation
- **Exit**: Return to mean + small profit (0.1-0.3%)
- **Hold Time**: 1-5 minutes
- **Win Rate**: 70-80%

### **3. Order Flow Scalping**
- **Entry**: Order book imbalance > 30% with price movement
- **Exit**: Imbalance reversal or profit target
- **Hold Time**: 10 seconds to 1 minute
- **Win Rate**: 60-70%

### **4. Market Making**
- **Strategy**: Provide liquidity on both sides
- **Profit**: Capture bid-ask spread
- **Risk**: Inventory management
- **Return**: Consistent small profits
